About

I started as a high performance coach, moved into biomechanics research, and fell in love with data science. I specialize in turning human pose data into actionable biomechanical insights using predictive models, spatiotemporal analysis, and machine learning. I believe that these tools can provide athletes and coaches with an unfair advantage.
