This project developed a bidirectional LSTM pipeline that converts 2D MediaPipe keypoints into 3D coordinates from a single camera view. The system achieved best performance at the 2 o'clock camera angle with an RMSE of approximately 2.97 cm.

The pipeline includes Optuna hyperparameter optimization, weighted loss functions for foot markers, sliding window inference with overlap blending, and interpretability via Integrated Gradients and SHAP.
